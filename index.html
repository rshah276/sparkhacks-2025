Pop-Up Connect

Welcome To Pop-Up Connect!

As the e-commerce space gets clouded by drop shippers and large corporations, it's important for creaters and curators to have physical spaces to vend directly to their communities. Pop-Up Connect is an interface for cafes, stores, and other businesses to post vendor market opportunities that other small businesses like crocheters, jewelry makers, and even vintage resellers can sign up for. Vendors can table at the venues and the businesses can get some extra foot traffic. There is also an interface for consumers to find markets to explore in their area!
